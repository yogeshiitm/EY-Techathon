Phase 1
  • Focus on Health:
    In first phase our main focus is on vaccinating the HealthCare personnel and Cleanliness Workers
Phase 2
  • Focus on Health:
    • 65+ and medium-Extreme Comorbidities
  • Focus on Economy
    • Frontline Essential Workers - Public Safety, Emergency, Farmers, etc
    • Economy Uplifting - Essential items manufacturing workers
Phase 3
  • Focus on Health
    • People with mild comorbidities
  • Focus on Economy Uplifting
    • Economy Uplifting - People working non remotely in sectors essential to GDP (ordered according to their illness score).
  • Focus on Reducing Disparity
    • Domestic Cleaners, Helper and other personal services workers essential to run Households. (ordered according to their illness score).
Phase 4:
  • All other people prioritized according to their illness score with more priority given to those who work/need to travel non remotely/ On-field over those who can stay/work at home/remotely.
